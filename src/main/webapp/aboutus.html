iCloudECG was developed as part of a growing initiative to streamline common aspects of diagnostics and healthcare in order to make these processes more efficient, economical, and accessible.

Our intelligent platform offers patients convenient insight into possible cardiac health complications, enabling a more informed plan of action in seeking medical diagnosis and treatment. iCloudECG also offers a centralized system for medical professionals to analyze and store multiple patient ECG records, giving professional users access to advanced data analysis tools.

Users simply upload ECG data sets to iCloudECG, and the system analyzes the data to identify: 1) The quality of the signal, and 2) characteristics indicative of cardiac abnormalities.
